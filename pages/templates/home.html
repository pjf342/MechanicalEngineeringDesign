Cylindrical Pressure Vessel Stresses

The pressure vessel can be capped or uncapped. Stresses can be calculated on the inner or outer walls.

A carbonated drink is an example of a capped/closed pressure vessel, while a gun barrel is an example of an uncapped/open pressure vessel.

If the vessel is closed/capped, there will be an axial stress.
